A new year brings a fresh perspective. Rather than setting broad resolutions that fade by February, I prefer to lay down a clear theme for my work, learning, and community contributions.

For 2026, my theme is: **Consistent Execution.**

Here is the breakdown of what I am focusing on this year.

## Building Useful Software

I want to spend less time reading about building and more time shipping. I am focusing on three core projects:
1. **Idea Thattukada:** A tool to capture and organize ideas quickly while browsing.
2. **NutriDecide:** A product aimed at assisting with nutrition and diet decisions.
3. **Otium:** A Flutter-based cognitive focus app featuring sprint timers and breathing resets.

## Learning in Public

Learning in public is one of the most effective ways to solidify knowledge. This year, my learning goals are:
- **Rust:** Understanding memory safety and concurrency concepts.
- **Next.js & Server Components:** Mastering the App Router model and performance optimizations.
- **Generative AI:** Deepening my understanding of prompt engineering and LLM application integrations.

## Empowering Communities

Community is the heartbeat of my growth. I will continue my work with **TinkerHub** and campus chapters. Specifically:
- **Mentorship sprints:** Organizing 1-on-1 and team mentoring formats.
- **Hackathons:** Scoping and curating experiential workshops.
- **Open-source onboarding:** Creating accessible paths for students to start building.

I'll be updating this blog as I learn and build. Let's make this year count!
